--- layout: page title: Research permalink: /research/ --- Unrolling FISTA to solve Low-Rank related problems

Abstract: Aided by the accessable of BP Algorithm for SVD operator, it is possible to unroll ISTA for low-rank regularization problems. To accelerate the convergence rate while avoiding the design of the momentum term manually, we come up with a structure similar to LSTM aiming at learning the momentum term adaptively. This method also gives an inspiration to unroll RPCA into deep neural network by disentangling RPCA into Low-Rank problem and Sparse Problem.


K-log: A robust clustering algorithm

Abstract: K-means is a common stretegy for clustering, thus, it is both efficient for computation and effective for it's convergence. Nonetheless, the adoptation of L2 norm as distance makes K-means sensitive to outliers. K-mediods is proposed to be more robust and based on the L1 norm, whlile making the algorithm more computational expensive. We come up with K-log, which combine the high efficiency of K-means and robustness of K-medoids
